About Me

Hi, I'm Phong Truong, a dedicated and passionate individual with a strong commitment to personal and professional growth. I believe in the power of determination and resilience to overcome challenges and achieve success.

Interests

Technology

I love the ever evolving technology that comes out and the way it makes a positive impact in our lives. I lived through the dial up days of old and get to appreciate the high speed internet of the present. The technological changes from flip phones to smart phones, from NES to the PS5, from manually rolling up your car windows to self-driving electric cars never cease to amaze me. I am truly in awe about the science of technology. I am also extremely grateful that I get to be working at HP and to be a part of innovation in bringing new technology to the people.
